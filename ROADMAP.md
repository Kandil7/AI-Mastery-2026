## Risk Management

### Technical Risks
| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Dependency breaking changes | High | Medium | Pin versions, test regularly |
| Performance regression | Medium | Medium | Benchmark CI, profiling |
| Security vulnerabilities | High | Low | Regular audits, dependabot |

### Community Risks
| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Contributor burnout | Medium | Medium | Rotate maintainers, recognize contributions |
| Toxic behavior | High | Low | Code of conduct, active moderation |
| Loss of momentum | Medium | Medium | Regular releases, community engagement |
